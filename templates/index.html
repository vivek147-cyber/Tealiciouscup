About us

Welcome to Tealicious Cup , your perfect destination for all your rejuvenating hangouts with friends and family ,started in January 2022 by Diyanshu Srivastava and Nikhil Pandey , with an fresh ambience situated in the heart of Lucknow ,at gomti nagar. Our customers can choose from over various options of Multi-flavoured tea brewed to perfection to delicious snacks with refreshing non-alcoholic drinks and scrumptious milk shakes from our delicious menu. All our dishes are made fresh and are mouth watering. Parties, Hangouts or just chilling with friends … Tealicious Cup is a place to mingle with friends ,unwind, relax and treasure the sweet things in life . We want to make sure that your visit to Tealicious Cup is more than just a meal; it is a unique experience that keeps you coming back for more.
